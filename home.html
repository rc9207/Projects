About The Site

I originally started with a traditional two dimensional website. While working with transformations in CSS, I suddenly realized that I could accomplish much more than I had originally intended. The end result of my efforts is the three dimensional cube on which my website is displayed on. This "Cube" is an combination of 6 seperate pages. All of which are rotated to the proper viewing angle. This gives the illusion that the website is actually three dimensional. finally I had set the the CSS rotation transformations into a singular JavaScript variable. Each transformation is governed by a single "Radio" button. In the future I plan on relocating the buttons to either side of the page with a clickable "arrow" image.
